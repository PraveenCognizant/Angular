Services & Dependency Injection

Interview Key Points:
  • @Injectable({ providedIn: 'root' }) = one instance for the whole app (singleton)
  • Services are injected via inject(ServiceName) or constructor
  • Services are NOT in the component's imports: [] array
  • Services keep business logic separate from UI components
